Privacy Policy

kaimono_labo — Aireal Inc.  |  Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Aireal Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the kaimono_labo application (the "Service"), a lifestyle product recommendation service that publishes content on TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with the Service.

2. Information We Collect

The Service collects minimal data through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 authentication, limited to our own company TikTok account only:

Data Type Source Purpose
Open ID TikTok OAuth Identify the authorized TikTok account for content posting
Display Name TikTok OAuth (user.info.basic scope) Verify the correct account is connected
Avatar URL TikTok OAuth (user.info.basic scope) Display in our internal dashboard for account verification

Important: We do not collect personal data from TikTok viewers or end users. The OAuth authentication is used exclusively for our own company account (@kaimono_labo) to publish content.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected through TikTok OAuth is used solely for:

  • Authenticating our TikTok account to enable automated content posting
  • Refreshing access tokens to maintain the publishing connection
  • Verifying account identity in our internal management dashboard

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens (access token, refresh token) are stored locally on our secure server
  • Tokens are encrypted at rest and never transmitted to third parties
  • Access to stored tokens is restricted to authorized system administrators
  • We do not store tokens in publicly accessible locations or version control systems

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose any collected data to third parties. The data is used exclusively for the purpose of publishing content to our own TikTok account.

6. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party platform:

  • TikTok (ByteDance) — Content publishing via the TikTok Content Posting API. TikTok's data practices are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only for as long as necessary to maintain the content publishing connection. If the Service is discontinued or the TikTok account is disconnected, all stored tokens will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

Since the Service operates on our own account and does not collect end-user personal data, there is no personal data of viewers to access, modify, or delete. If you believe we have inadvertently collected your personal data, please contact us and we will promptly address your concern.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI)
  • TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Data Processing requirements
